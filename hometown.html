Haryana

History

After revolt of 1857, in April 1858 Haryana, then known as Delhi Territory, was merged
with Punjab Province as a punishment. During the British Colonial period, from 1858 to
1947 it was administered as a part of the Punjab province. It became a separate
administrative state of India in 1966.
